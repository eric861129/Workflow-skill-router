## Responsibility Labels

- **Release authority**: may approve V2 tags, release notes, channel movement, and publication after the release gate passes.
- **Security triage**: may receive private reports and coordinate remediation or advisories.
- **Evaluation review**: may attest whether behavior evidence is complete, reproducible, and safe to publish.

Repository automation is not a maintainer and cannot grant release, security, evaluation, or model-quota approval.
